SmartDoc Automator 🧠📄

SmartDoc Automator is a powerful AI-driven tool that transforms how you interact with digital content. Whether you're uploading a PDF, scanned image, audio clip, or video — SmartDoc extracts the text using OCR, speech-to-text, or video transcription. It then lets you merge, summarize, analyze, and export your data with ease.

✨ Features

  • Supports .txt, .pdf, .jpg, .mp3, .mp4 and more
  • OCR with Tesseract, speech-to-text, and video captioning
  • Summarizes using NLP and generates .docx reports
  • Word count, AI-powered summaries, and downloadable outputs
  • Dark-themed, clean UI with responsive design

🛠️ Tech Stack

Python, Flask, HTML, TailwindCSS, OpenCV, Tesseract OCR, MoviePy, HuggingFace Transformers
