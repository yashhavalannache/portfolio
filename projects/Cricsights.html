Cricsights 🏏📊

Cricsights is a powerful cricket analytics and strategic planning platform built for players, analysts, coaches, and die-hard fans who want to go beyond the scorecard. This full-stack web application breaks down the game with precision — combining data science, intuitive visualizations, and deep cricket knowledge into a single intelligent dashboard.

  • Player Search: Access player profiles enriched with stats, performance trends, strengths, weaknesses, and preferred zones.
  • Batsman vs Bowler Matchups: Dive into detailed one-on-one analysis — from average strike rates to total dismissals and clutch performance zones.
  • Role-Based Comparison: Compare opening batsmen, death-over bowlers, or spinners based on metrics like economy, scoring zones, consistency, and match impact.
  • Venue-Wise Performance: Understand how players perform across pitches and stadiums with visual graphs and location-based filters.
  • Team Room: A digital strategy board where team captains and coaches can simulate matchups, define field placements, record pre-match notes, and prepare bowling spells — just like a real dressing room whiteboard.

Cricsights isn’t just a tool, it’s a tactical advantage. Whether you're building a fantasy team, preparing for a college match, or studying international player trends — this platform brings together performance intelligence and a clean, intuitive UI to help you make better, bolder, and sharper decisions.

✨ Features

  • Player search with detailed stats, strengths, and weaknesses
  • Batsman vs Bowler matchup analysis with graphs
  • Role-based comparison tools (openers, pacers, etc.)
  • Team Room: plan strategies, field placements, and notes
  • Fully responsive UI for fast insights before every match

🛠️ Tech Stack

Python, Flask, Pandas, Matplotlib, HTML/CSS, Tailwind, Cricket Data APIs
